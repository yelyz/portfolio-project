Main instrument

My main instrument is electric guitar. It's the first instrument I've learned, the one I have the most experience with, and coincidentally the one I enjoy playing the most. I am a guitarist for a beginner band.

Other skills

Digital skills include recording music (vocals and intrumentals), mixing and mastering tracks. For my work I use DAWs (I prefer FL Studio, although I also have experience with Reaper and Adobe Audition) and a number of different plugins. Moreover, I have some experience with creating sounds from scratch with a synth.

Non-digital skills include beginner-level piano playing, as well as some technical knowledge about guitars, mics and studio equipment.

Events

My experience with real-life events as an artist is currently limited, but I am working on expanding it. I've played a couple songs at a small gig in Kyiv with my previous band, and it was quite successful. I am looking forward to playing more gigs and gaining experience!
